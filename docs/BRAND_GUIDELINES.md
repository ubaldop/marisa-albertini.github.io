# Brand Guidelines - Marisa Albertini

This document outlines the brand identity, visual design system, and design guidelines for Marisa Albertini's professional practice.

## Brand Introduction

### Positioning
Marisa Albertini guides her patients toward acquiring new skills.

**How does she do it?**
Through personalized intervention strategies in an inclusive and welcoming environment where every individual can face their challenges and achieve well-being for themselves and their families.

### Core Values
- **Equity** - Fair and equal treatment for all individuals
- **Empathy** - Understanding and connecting with patients' experiences
- **Responsibility** - Commitment to professional excellence and ethical practice
- **Inclusivity** - Creating welcoming spaces for diverse individuals

### About
Marisa Albertini is a Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA). She specializes in neurodevelopmental disorders, primarily working with autistic individuals, and her main focus is on children, though she also frequently collaborates with adolescents and some adults.

### Brand Promise
**Charting routes toward the acquisition of new skills.**

## Logo - Concept

Marisa Albertini's professional figure is like a constellation that illuminates and guides the journey of travelers.

The goal of the logo is to symbolize the orientation, clarity, and support that Marisa Albertini offers to her patients. A monogram that becomes a constellation and also represents her ability to connect with others and guide them through unknown territories.

This brand embodies her promise to be a reliable and luminous guide on the path toward her patients' personal successes.

## Color Palette

The chosen colors are warm and reassuring, taking inspiration from the colors that can be observed in galaxies. They reflect the nature of Marisa Albertini's brand: professional, vibrant, free, empathetic, and attractive.

### Primary Colors

| Color      | Hex Code  | Usage                                    |

| Yellow     | `#f6ad00` | Primary accent, call-to-action buttons   |
| Red        | `#ff4545` | Secondary accent, emphasis               |

| Color      | Hex Code  | Usage                                    |
|------------|-----------|------------------------------------------|
| Gray 01    | `#353534` | Primary text, dark elements              |
| Gray 02    | `#666666` | Secondary text                           |

## Typography

### Primary Typeface
**Karla** (ExtraBold - Regular)

The primary typeface for all content, ensuring clean readability and modern professionalism.

### Secondary Typeface
**Sansita Swashed** (Black - Regular)

A decorative typeface to be used sparingly for visual interest.

### Font Usage Guidelines

#### Sansita Swashed
**Available weights:** Black | ExtraBold | Bold | SemiBold | Medium | Regular | Light

**Usage:**
- Consider this a **service font** - use it minimally
- Function: Breaking element, visual attention grabber
- Primary use: Titles in posters and social media posts
- Website use: Occasional accent to prevent wall of text
- **NEVER** use as body text - only for titles or subtitles
- Alternative substitute: Sansita

#### Karla ExtraBold
**Usage:** Main titles and headings

#### Karla Bold
**Usage:** Subtitles at 40-50% of the title size

#### Karla SemiBold / Medium
**Usage:** Body copy at 20-30% of the title size

### Typography Best Practices

**Line Spacing**
- May vary based on design needs
- Should **never exceed 130%** of the text size

**Capitalization**
- Use uppercase text **only for Call to Action** elements
- Maintain sentence case for body text and most headings

**Readability Standards**
- Print media: Body text should not be smaller than **6pt**
- Digital media: Body text should not be smaller than **10-12px**

## Design Principles

1. **Warmth & Reassurance** - Use the galaxy-inspired color palette to create welcoming, calming experiences
2. **Clarity & Guidance** - Like a constellation, the design should orient and guide users clearly
3. **Professional Excellence** - Maintain clean, modern design that reflects BCBA certification and expertise
4. **Inclusivity** - Design with accessibility in mind for diverse audiences
5. **Visual Balance** - Use the service font (Sansita Swashed) sparingly to break monotony without overwhelming

## Application Examples

### Website
- Primary backgrounds: White or Light Beige (`#FFF3E7`)
- Headers: Karla ExtraBold in Indigo (`#4228af`)
- Body text: Karla Regular/Medium in Gray 01 (`#353534`)
- CTAs: Yellow (`#f6ad00`) with uppercase Karla Bold
- Accents: Use Red, Turquoise for visual interest

### Social Media
- Headers: Mix of Karla ExtraBold and Sansita Swashed (sparingly)
- Backgrounds: Galaxy-inspired gradients using primary colors
- Text overlays: White on dark backgrounds, Dark Gray on light backgrounds

### Print Materials
- Business cards: Constellation logo with Indigo and Yellow
- Brochures: Light Beige backgrounds with primary color accents
- Letterhead: Minimal design with logo and contact in Gray 01
